Integration Plans

We are integrating with multiple financial institutions to offer flexible savings options:

  • Mpesa: Save money directly through Mpesa Paybill/Till numbers.
  • KCB: Use KCB's API to integrate with your savings account.
  • Equity: Integration with Equity Bank for seamless transactions.
  • National Bank: Direct savings and transactions through National Bank's API.

How to Save

Saving with Jaza Savings is easy and secure:

  1. Create an Account: Sign up on our platform and complete your profile.
  2. Link Your Bank Accounts: Add and verify your Mpesa, KCB, Equity, and National Bank accounts.
  3. Set Savings Goals: Define your savings targets and schedule automatic transfers.
  4. Monitor Your Savings: Track your savings progress through our dashboard.
  5. Withdraw Funds: Easily withdraw your savings when needed.
